INDUSTRIAL & UTILITY PROJECTS

The regulations surrounding utility projects and the challenges facing network stability in industrial projects were the main two obstacles that we targeted to overcome when undertaking the design.

Extensive power system studies and network stability analysis were conducted for these projects to ensure safe and reliable operation. Design involved different systems that need to be applied and coordinated.

Projects done in this sector include;
• Al-Samra Waste Water Treatment Plant – Jordan
• Catico Motors Factory – Egypt
• Dreamland Power Station – Egypt
• Al-Koraymat 66/11kV Power Station – Egypt
